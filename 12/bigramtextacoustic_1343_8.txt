He added in a little while, "go yonder to my brother and tell him. If he withdraw his troops, he shall be earl of northumberland and rich and powerful in england. The captain rode away and gave the message." 
"What will he give to my friend the king of norway," asked the brother.
"Seven feet of earth for a grave," replied the captain. 
"No more," returned the brother with a smile. 
"The king of norway being a tall man perhaps a little more," replied the captain. 
"Ride back," said the brother, and tell king harold to make ready for the fight. He did so very soon.
