Boiled perch with oyster sauce prepare and clean the fish and simmer until done in salted and acidulated water, drain and serve with oyster sauce. Perch a l'allemande put two large cleaned perch into a saucepan with two chopped carrots. A sprig of parsley, a celery root, a sliced onion and a pinch of salt cover with white wine and simmer for twenty minutes. Drain and keep warm take out the onion parsley and celery root add half a cupful of chopped mushrooms and cook for five minutes. Cook with a tablespoonful each of butter and flour thickened together. Take from the fire ,add a tablespoonful of butter, and the juice of two lemons pour over the fish and serve.
