But no. On reflection the procureur was not a merciless man, and it was not the magistrate slave to his duties but the friend, the loyal friend who roughly but firmly cut into the very core of the corruption. It was not the executioner but the surgeon who wished to withdraw the honor of danglars from ignominious association with the disgraced young man they had presented to the world as their son in law. And since villefort the friend of danglars had acted in this way, no one could suppose that he had been previously acquainted with or had lent himself to any of andrea's intrigues.
